Here’s a concise product/landing-page brief you can hand to yourself or a designer/dev.

***

## 1. Product concept

A Gmail-connected **job search cockpit** for laid‑off mid‑career professionals that acts like a personal recruiter: organizing recruiter emails, tracking applications, and drafting tailored resumes, cover letters, and follow‑ups — all invisible to employers.

**Core idea:**  
“Your private recruiter-grade control center for your job search, built on top of your existing Gmail.”

***

## 2. Target user

- Laid‑off mid‑career professionals (approx. 30–55).  
- Have 8–20+ years experience, often in tech/knowledge work.  
- Currently hunting for a similar or adjacent role, feeling overwhelmed by:  
  - Recruiter email chaos  
  - Constant resume/cover-letter tailoring  
  - Tracking dozens of applications and follow-ups manually  

**Primary pains to call out:**

- “My inbox is a mess of recruiters, job alerts, and automated rejections.”  
- “Customizing my resum role is exhausting.”  
- “I lose track of where I’ve applied and when I should follow up.”  
- “I’m worried employers will judge me if they know I’m using AI.”

***

## 3. Value proposition

**One-liner (for hero section):**  
“Your invisible recruiter for your job search — built on top of Gmail.”

**Supporting promise:**  
“Connect your Gmail and we’ll organize your job search, tailor your outreach, and keep you on track — without employers ever seeing our name.”

**Key benefits:**

1. **Clarity:** Turn recruiter emails and job alerts into a clean pipeline of applications and next steps.  
2. **Leverage:** AI-generated resumes, cover letters, and follow-up emails tailored to each role and your voice.  
3. **Stealth:** All messages come from your existing email; no “sent via X” branding.  
4. **Momentum:** Daily plan with exactly what to apply to, who to follow up with, and how.

***

## 4. Core features to describe

You’re not promising a full product yet, just the **ined inbox (on top of Gmail)**  
   - Auto-detect recruiter and employer emails, group them by company and role.  
   - Mark each thread as an “Application” with a stage (saved, applied, interview, offer).  

2. **Application dashboard**  
   - Kanban/board view of all applications: company, role, stage, last contact, next action.  
   - Quick filters: “needs follow-up,” “interview scheduled,” “waiting on response.”  

3. **AI resumes & cover letters**  
   - Paste a job description or link; get a tailored resume variant and cover letter.  
   - Optimized for ATS and mid‑career positioning, with your tone and strengths.  

4. **AI email assistant (stealth)**  
   - Drafts outreach, thank-you notes, and follow-ups from your real email.  
   - Suggests replies inside each thread; you review and send with one click.  

5. **Calendar + prep**  
   - Detects interview invites, adds them to your calendar.  
   - Generates prep notes: company overview, suggested stories, and questions to ask.
